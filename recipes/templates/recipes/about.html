About Recipe Engine

Your intelligent companion in the kitchen.

Welcome to Recipe Engine – your intelligent companion in the kitchen! Our platform is designed to help you discover delicious recipes tailored to your taste, dietary needs, and available ingredients.

🔍 What We Do

Recipe Engine is a dynamic search tool that lets you explore thousands of recipes sourced from the powerful Edamam API. Whether you're cooking with limited ingredients or searching for healthy meal options, we help you find the right recipe in seconds.

🛠 Built With Modern Technologies

  • •Django – for handling our robust backend, routing, and template rendering
  • •Edamam API – to fetch and serve real-time recipe data, nutritional information, and more
  • •HTML5, CSS3, Tailwind CSS – to deliver a responsive and user-friendly front end

💡 Why Use Recipe Engine?

  • •Search recipes by ingredients, diet, or cuisine
  • •Get step-by-step cooking instructions
  • •Save time and reduce food waste
  • •Discover new meal ideas tailored to your needs

🤝 Our Mission

Our mission is to empower home cooks of all skill levels to enjoy cooking, eat healthier, and make the most of what they have in their kitchens.
